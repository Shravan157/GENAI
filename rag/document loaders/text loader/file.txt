The City After Rain

The city looked different after the rain. The roads were covered with small puddles, and the streetlights reflected on the wet ground like golden lines. People walked carefully, holding umbrellas and avoiding splashes from passing vehicles.

Near a small tea stall, a group of friends stood together, laughing and sharing hot cups of tea. The smell of fresh rain mixed with the aroma of ginger tea, making the evening feel peaceful. Even though the day had been busy and noisy, the rain gave everything a calm and refreshing feeling.

Sometimes, simple moments like these remind us to slow down and notice the world around us.
